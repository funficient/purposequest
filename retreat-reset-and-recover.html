7-Day Journey Program

This package is crafted to let you shape it exactly to your needs. Your journey, your way.

Location: Antbear Eco Lodge, Drakensberg

Join us for an intensive self-discovery program, combining a 5-day coaching program with relaxation and nurturing at your own pace. The perfect place for healing, this program is for those wanting big change, fast.

At the end of the week you will have gained more clarity on your purpose, worked through the first layers of resistance, and gained valuable tools to continue this "work" at home, supported by an individual coaching journal.
