3-Day Journey Program

This package is crafted to let you shape it exactly to your needs. Your journey, your way.

Location: Antbear Eco Lodge, Drakensberg

Join us for an intensive self-discovery program, combining coaching with relaxation and nurturing at your own pace. The perfect place for healing, this program is for those wanting a change in perspective or renewed focus.

At the end of the week you will have gained more clarity on your purpose or next steps, worked through the first layers of resistance that keeps you from achieving your goal, and clarity on your next steps.
