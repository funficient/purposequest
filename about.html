Guiding principles

Quiet

Step into silence to listen for the whisper of your soul and the wisdom of nature.

Connection

When we look for how we are similar, trying to understand the other, it's not possible to hate or hurt another.

Growth

We're always evolving, becoming more. We aim to add to personal growth in some way, shape, or form.

Authenticity

No perfection required. Connection can only happen when you can allow yourself to be vulnerable.

Impact

Knowledge not applied is incomplete. We aim for action and impact to create ripple effects of change.

Zero waste

We always look for ways to reduce, reuse and recycle in creative ways to reduce our carbon footprint.
