3-Day Journey Program to Reconnect With Your Divine Feminine

Location: Antbear Eco Lodge, Drakensberg

Step away from the noise and into the stillness of the Drakensberg mountains for a weekend of reconnecting with the goddess within. Held at the peaceful Antbear Lodge, this retreat blends the emotional, physical, and practical aspects of divine femininity in an inspiring natural setting.

Through guided conversations, soulful exploration, and gentle movement, you’ll redefine what feminine power means to you and plant the seed for a divine expression of femininity.
