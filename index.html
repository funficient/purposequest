Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our retreats.

How are your retreats different from other wellness programs?

We believe that you are your own best teacher, and our role is to merely facilitate your journey of self-discovery. Our approach focus on a fully immersive epxerience, supported by top coaches and facilitators, nutritious farm-to-table food, and ample space to breathe and connect with nature in a safe and nurturing place.

What is included in the price?

The retreat price includes accommodation, all meals, guided sessions, group activities, and follow-up resources. Transportation to the retreat location is not included but we can help arrange carpools. Extras such as massages or drinks from the bar are available at an additional cost.

What kind of accommodation and facilities are available?

There are eighteen spacious and private units, each able to house two individuals, with the possibility for twin beds. Each unit has its own bathroom. There is also a large communial area where meals are enjoyed with a lounge area to relax either outside on the patio or upstairs. The farm is a fully functional farm with cows, horses, chickens, a small vegetable garden, and ample space to go on nature walks and breathe the fresh air.

How many participants attend each retreat?

We keep our retreats intimate with 4-12 participants maximum. This allows for personalized attention, meaningful connections, and a safe space for vulnerability and growth within the group setting.

Is the food quality and menu suitable for our group’s needs?

All our food are freshly prepared according to your dietary requirements, much of it sourced either from our own farm, or from the local community. We have a standard farmhouse breakfast, and a pre-planned three course meal for dinner.
