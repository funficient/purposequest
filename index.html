The need-to-know stuff

The Essentials

All the most asked questions answered. Still have questions? Contact us.

Who is this for?

▼

All our retreats are designed for anyone seeking clarity of purpose, inspiration, or deep rest while connecting with the surrounding natural beauty. If you are navigating a big life transition or looking for more creative ways to heal, our retreats are for you.

What will I take away?

▼

Expect more clarity and alignment with your purpose, practical tools you can take home to continue your healing journey, renewed focus, and meaningful connections. You will find yourself making different choices and lining up with your desired outcome with little to no effort as the deep work will change your mindset around your goals.

How do I get there and around?

▼

The closest international airport is King Shaka International Airport (DUR) in Durban. It is 2.5 hours away from the venue. Johannesburg is a 5 hour drive and the closest local airport is Pietermaritzburg.

Public transport is limited and unreliable. It is recommended to rent a car for the duration of your trip at the airport, or you can book an airport transfer.

The last few kilometers to the lodge are a gravel road; however, it is not necessary to have a 4x4, and easily accessible with any type of car. There is ample parking available on site.

There are currently roadworks, so please be patient!

Tell me more about the accommodation.

▼

Located at the foothills of Giant's Castle, Antbear Eco Lodge is about 40 minutes drive from Estcourt, the nearest town. There are 15 private units sleeping two to four people, each with a fireplace, lounge area and spacious bathroom with shower and bathtub. There is a coffee station and bar fridge and all the amenities to make your stay comfortable. Here, comfort meets craftsmanship in a setting that feels like a home away from home. Designed with an artistic flair, every piece of wooden furniture is handcrafted by Antbear Lodge's owner, making each unit truly unique and inspiring.

The mountain view units have the more exquisite view over the surrounding mountain range and lakes, while the garden view untis have views over the spacious and open gardens. Each unit is designed around privacy, and even when fully booked, you will feel as if you are alone.

The main lodge area has wifi and ample space for working or relaxing both inside and outside on the patio. There is also a bar area and a upper lounge area with comfortable sofas.

All units are serviced daily. Visit

www.antbear.com

for more details.

What food will we eat?

▼

All meals are included in your stay and cater to different dietary requirements. We produce as much as possible of the produce on the farm. Fresh from our farm and to the dining table we serve eggs, vegetables and jam.

Breakfast consists of fresh fruit salad, yoghurt, muesli, cornflakes, fruit juice, milk, freshly baked bread, pastries, tea, coffee, homemade jams and cheese. You can order eggs any way you like them. We also offer bacon, sausages, fried tomatoes, mushrooms and baked beans, depending on availability.

Lunch and dinner changes daily reflecting a wide variety of typical South African and European cuisine.

What is included in the price?

▼

All prices quoted are all-inclusive and includes accommodation, meals, guided sessions, group activities, and follow-up resources.

Transportation to the retreat location and any extras such as add-on massages or drinks from the bar is not included and available at an additional cost.

Can I bring my pet?

▼
Yes! This is one of the best pet-friendly places in the Drakensberg. Your dog will be happy with lots of dams to swim in and vast open fields to run around. There is no additional cost for pets, we just ask that you take full responsibility for caring for your pet.



Some of the units are fenced. While we don't require your pet to be on leash, it's recommended to put your pet on a lead upon arrival to ensure they don't chase the chickens, cows, horses and donkeys. Please bring your own dog food, bowls, and bedding.

Featured in www.pethealthcare.co.za. The Fastest Growing Pet-Friendly website in South Africa www.PetHealthCare.co.za

What are visa and other travel requirements?

▼

Most countries visiting South Africa don't need a visa of 90 day stay for business or tourism. It is advisable to confirm with the South African embassy or consulate. Some countries may face new entry requirements such as electronic travel authorizations (ETAs) for South Africa, but these are still being rolled out and may vary by country.
