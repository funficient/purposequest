Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our retreats.

How are your retreats different from other wellness programs?

We focus on wholeness and creating an immersive experience that includes movement, sounds, and getting in touch with your inner GPS to guide you through life. Joining a retreat is just the beginning. Our experiences contains practical tools that you can take back home to maintain the calm in your busy daily life.



Supported by top coaches and experienced facilitators, nutritious farm-to-table food, and ample space to breathe and connect with nature in a safe and nurturing place, you're in good hands.

What is included in the price?

The retreat price includes accommodation, all meals, guided sessions, group activities, and follow-up resources. Transportation to the retreat location is not included. Extras such as massages or drinks from the bar or any additional services are also excluded and available at an additional cost.

What kind of accommodation and facilities are available?

There are fifteen spacious and private units, each able to house two individuals, with the possibility for twin beds. Some of the units can sleep four people. Each unit has its own bathroom and lounge area with fireplace. There is also a large communial area at the lodge where meals are enjoyed with a lounge area to relax either upstairs or the patio outside. The farm is a functional farm with horses, cows, chickens, a small vegetable garden, and ample space to go on nature walks and breathe the fresh air.

How many participants attend each retreat?

We keep our retreats intimate with 4-12 participants maximum. This allows for personalized attention, meaningful connections, and a safe space for vulnerability and growth within the group setting.

Is the food quality and menu suitable for our group’s needs?

All our food are freshly prepared according to your dietary requirements, much of it sourced either from our own farm, or from the local community. We have a standard full farmhouse breakfast, and a delicious pre-planned three course meal for dinner.

Can I bring my pet?

Yes! This is one of the best pet-friendly places in the Drakensberg. Your dog will be happy with lots of dams to swim in and vast open fields to run around. There is no additional cost for pets, we just ask that you take full responsibility for caring for your pet.



Some of the accommodation units are fenced, allowing you to explore the region while your pets stay behind. While we don't require your pet to be on leash, it's recommended to put your pet on a lead upon arrival to ensure they don't chase the chickens, cows, horses and donkeys.



Please bring your own dog food, bowls and bedding.
