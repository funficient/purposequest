You don’t need to be a writer to have a story worth telling. 

This retreat is your invitation to slow down, breathe deeply, and rediscover the story you want to live, collaboratively.

Whether you’re a writer or simply curious about your next chapter, you’ll find inspiration, support, and connection here.

Guided Sessions

Guided sessions to choose your character, build a world together, and write a story of transformation.

Restorative Moments

Plenty of time to enjoy the beautiful views, connect with nature, and write at your own pace.

Optional Extras

Turn your story into a book with our network of professional editors, website designers, and publishers.

This retreat is for you if:

  • You're an aspiring or seasoned writer wanting to explore a collaborative story experience.
  • You're navigating a major transition in life and want to reset and recalibrate and design your own life story in a unique way.
  • You're successful by social standards but crave creative self-expression, community, and connection.
  • You’re seeking fresh inspiration to bring more curiosity, purpose, and joy into your life’s next chapter.
