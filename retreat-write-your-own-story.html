3-Day Journey Program to Your Next Chapter

Location: Antbear Eco Lodge, Drakensberg

Step away from the noise and into the stillness of the Drakensberg mountains for a weekend of reflection, creativity, and connection. This retreat blends storytelling, self-discovery, and purpose-finding in an inspiring natural setting.

Through guided writing, shared conversations, and soulful exploration, you’ll craft your own story and plant the seed for a more purpose-driven life. Perfect for anyone ready to reconnect, reset, and rediscover their path in a meaningful, creative way.
