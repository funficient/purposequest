About Purpose Quest

Guided creative coaching retreats in the Drakensberg that help you reset, reconnect, and move forward with clarity.

Our Vision

We believe work and life can be intentionally designed. Through immersive experiences in the mountains, we create space for reflection, creative expression, and purposeful action.

What We Do

  • Transformative solo and duo coaching retreats — deep resets that bring clarity and momentum
  • Group creative workshops combining coaching and art to unlock new perspectives
  • Conflict resolution and connection retreats tailored for couples and teams

Our offerings and formats are designed around immersive stays in the Drakensberg and the KwaZulu-Natal region.

Our Story

Born from a desire to turn life’s pauses into purposeful momentum, Purpose Quest started as simple creative coaching sessions and grew into weekend and multi-day retreats. We blend evidence-informed coaching, gentle facilitation, and creative practices to help people uncover what matters most.
