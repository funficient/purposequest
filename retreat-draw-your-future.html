Sacred Geometry Kintsugi.

A 3-day restorative weekend retreat in the Kwazulu Natal Midlands to heal and manifest with coaching, journalling, and sacred geometry.

What if your imperfections made you more beautiful? 

Kintsugi is the ancient Japanese art of joining broken pieces with gold, transforming broken pottery into something stronger, more valuable, and more beautiful than before. In this retreat, we'll use sacred geometry and coaching to transform your pain into power.

This retreat is for you if:

  • You're successful by social standards but crave creative self-expression, calm, and connection.
  • You're looking for a engaging weekend activity where you can heal in a loving and gentle way.
  • You’re seeking inspiration, joy, and a spark of creativity to guide you into your next chapter.
  • You're seeking clarity of purpose and a more meaningful and joyful life.

Guided sessions

Discover your soul’s calling through guided coaching and meditation enhanced by color therapy and the meditative power of sacred geometry.

Restorative moments

There is time for relaxation and movement with our morning mindful walkshops where you can enjoy the beautiful views and reconnect with the peace of being in nature.

Optional extras

Sign up for the Firestarters coaching program or art classes to continue your journey of self-expression and empowerment.

3-Day Journey Program to Your Next Dream

Location: Antbear Eco Lodge, Drakensberg

Step away from the noise and into the stillness of the Drakensberg for a weekend of creativity, connection, and manifestation. Hosted at Antbear Lodge, this retreat blends purpose-driven coaching facilitated by Kate, sacred geometry, color therapy and mindful time in nature.

Perfect for anyone ready to manifest a life they love in a creative way.

Program Schedule
Day 1

Arrive and settle in

Check-in anytime from 14.00pm and settle in.

We'll start off the journey in an introductory coaching session before dinner, establishing a baseline as well as identify the shift you hope to make during the program and what you want to walk away with.

After dinner you will be guided to the outdoor hammock camp with Mlindeni, where you can enjoy views of the uninterrupted starry sky as you lay in a hammock, listening to African folk tales which has been passed on through multiple generations, giving you insight into the Zulu culture while reminding you of your connection to the stars and your ancestors.

Day 2

Day of Transformation

We'll start the day with a mindful nature walk and add a breathwork and meditation routine to get your energy flowing correctly and prepare your body for the energetic shift.

After breakfast we'll start with an outdoor (weather permitting) coaching session where you will be guided through a gentle process through all the levels of your neurology to uncover the why behind a particular goal or desire for a purpose-aligned vision of a desired future you might have, whether it is a better relationship, a career focused goal, or simply gaining clarity as to what a happier life will look like for you.

After lunch you'll get a color reading and use these colors to draw and then paint a flower of life sacred geometry symbol. The evening is yours to reflect on a journal prompt.

Day 3

Preparing for the return

After our final morning practice and breakfast we'll have a wrap-up reflection to evaluate your shift before you head back home.
