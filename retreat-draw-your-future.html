3-Day Journey Program to Your Next Dream

Location: Antbear Eco Lodge, Drakensberg Dates: 24 - 26 Oct 2025 / 21 - 23 Nov 2025

Step away from the noise and into the stillness of the Drakensberg for a weekend of creativity, connection, and manifestation. Hosted at Antbear Lodge, this retreat blends purpose-driven coaching facilitated by Kate, and beginner-friendly art lessons facilitated by Mary-Jane, and mindful time in nature.

You’ll sketch and paint your vision for the future using pen and watercolor, turning it into a unique hand-painted vision board. Guided coaching will help you clarify your dream and identify the key actions to bring it to life, closing with a group reflection circle.

Perfect for anyone ready to manifest a life they love in a creative way while learning a new skill.
